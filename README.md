## Core Features
### Redstone & Survival Optimization
Leveraging mods like **Carpet**, **Tweakeroo**, and **MiniHUD**, this pack enhances redstone and survival gameplay. 
- With **Litematica**, easily construct large-scale builds. 
- **Tweakeroo** offers numerous utilities such as Freecam and scheduled attacks. 
- **Carpet** adds server-side bots, efficiency statistics, and more (requires the mod on the server).

### Smooth Animations
- Fluid interface transitions and scrolling
- Optimized GUI animations with responsive controls
- Refined visual feedback for an elevated experience

### Exceptional Performance
- Powered by modern optimization mods like **Sodium** and **Lithium**, ensure smooth performance in your world.
- Supports most OptiFine features, including shaders, connected textures, and zoom. If your resource pack states "OptiFine required," it will likely function here as well.

### Survival Assistance
The pack includes essential utilities like **JEI**, **Mouse Tweaks**, and **Auto Fishing+**, providing a wide range of gameplay aids.

### Multiplayer Support
With **ViaFabricPlus**, connect to servers across different versions with one client.
